What's Behind HMR's Immersive Experiences

Chicago's full-service luxury event design and production studio

By Vivienne Falls

For over 40 years, HMR Designs has been creating environments that change not just how an event looks, but how it feels from the moment guests walk in. Based in Chicago, the studio has built a world-renowned reputation by bringing ambitious concepts to life at weddings, galas, corporate events, mitzvahs, and private celebrations across the Midwest and around the world — always with the belief that no two should ever be the same.

The creative process begins with listening. Some clients arrive with a clear vision; others simply know the mood they want to evoke. Either way, HMR translates those ideas into a fully realized environment, guiding the project from concept through production. By the time an event comes to life, every detail has been considered from every angle.

HMR's designers draw from architecture, fashion, art, travel, and a wide range of cultural influences. They filter those references through each client's aesthetic to create something that couldn't exist for anyone else. The studio isn't chasing trends. It's building experiences shaped around the people they're designed for.

That approach is facilitated by the specialized range of disciplines working collaboratively under one roof. HMR brings together designers, fabricators, floral artists, lighting specialists, and production teams from the very outset. Ideas are developed, built and managed internally, rather than being entrusted to subcontractors. This meticulous control ensures that every element comes together seamlessly on the most critical day, creating a beautifully harmonious environment where each component has rightfully earned its place.

Florals have always been at the heart of HMR's work. With decades of botanical expertise across the team, flowers establish scale, movement, and emotion within a space — whether through intimate tabletop arrangements, sculptural installations, or sweeping compositions that transform an entire room. Often, they're the starting point for everything that follows.

From there, statement lighting, custom-built structures, specialty furnishings, graphics, and unexpected materials work alongside florals to shape the full experience. Every element is considered in relation to the next, creating environments that feel layered, immersive, and unmistakably personal. It's an approach that has made HMR a premier studio for one-of-a-kind event design, from a Chicago ballroom to a destination beach wedding. The setting changes. The standard doesn't.
